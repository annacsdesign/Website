Design Days 2018
In my last quarter at WWU, I was asked to create the branding for the university's annual design week. I worked with design faculty and student representatives to develop an identity system for the event. Design Days offered a wide range of opportunities for students to get involved with the department and learn about contemporary design dialogues. This year's events included a screening of Stefan Sagmeister's The Happy Film and live screenprinting and letterpress demonstrations.
