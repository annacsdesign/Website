Klipsun
Klipsun is an award-winning student publication highlighting long-form journalism and beautiful photography. I served as a designer for Klipsun's winter 2018 issue before rising to the role of lead designer for the spring and fall issues.The magazine has a rotating staff, which causes it to have a different journalistic focus each quarter, making exciting work for a design team.
