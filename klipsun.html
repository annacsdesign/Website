Klipsun is an award-winning publi­cation high­lighting long-form jour­nalism and beautiful photo­graphy.
I designed for three issues of Klipsun, including serving as lead designer on the Essence and Changemaker issues. Each magazine has a new staff and journalistic focus, resulting in tonal shifts in the visual design from issue to issue.
