How the World Sees You
A hypothetical book design project asked me to recreate the cover of Sally Hogshead's How the World Sees You. Readers are guided to determine their own strengths and use them to achieve personal and business success, based off their natural characteristics.

My cover for How the World Sees You was inspired by the patterns used in colorblindness tests. The challenge was to create a cover that was suitably professional but interesting enough to draw the eye from a bookshelf.
