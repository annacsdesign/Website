So nice to meet you!
I'm Anna: visual designer, Seattle native, and friend. My work has focused primarily on the arts, editorial design, and education.

I graduated with a degree in design and a minor in art history from Western Washington University. Since 2019, I have been a graphic designer at Seattle Art Museum, creating exhibition graphics, campaign creative, signage, and much more at the Pacific Northwest's leading visual arts institution. In the past I was the sole visual designer for Penwizard Personalized Books, oversaw the design and printing of several WWU campus publications, and interned at WatchGuard Technologies and Bellevue Arts Museum. After work I can be found cooking through the Bon Appetit back catalogue, going to concerts, or reading on public transit.

Currently accepting freelance work. Drop me a line if you'd like to make something happen.
