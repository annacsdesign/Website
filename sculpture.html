INFO WORK
An exercise in anima­tion and illus­tration showcas­ing a beloved collec­tion of art.
Despite the reknown of the artists in WWU’s sculpture collection, the structures’ minimal forms and neutral colors mean they fade into the background of the campus. Several of the most well-known pieces are often sat on, walked over, or swung from by students. I used this theme to created a short video reimagining the collection as a sort of playground made from bright, blocky colors and shapes, highlighting some of my favorite works from my own time at WWU.
